Welcome to BodyFlex

ABOUT US



The gym of the "BodyFlex" sports complex, located on the first floor of the building, has an area of ​​165 sq.m. and is intended for general physical and strength training. The hall is equipped with a modern air conditioning system. It provides a constant circulation of fresh air, so that classes are held with maximum comfort at any time of the year. The gym is equipped with American simulators of the CYBEX company - a leader in the production of simulators, as well as video and audio equipment, which creates an excellent atmosphere for the training process.

Our Services



CrossFit

It`s an extreme system of general physical training based on alternating, sometimes randomly, basic movements from various sports (kettlebell lifting, weightlifting, artistic gymnastics, rowing, athletics, etc.), performed with high intensity.



Gym

A specially equipped room, including inventory, and designed for training, sports games, sports or physical education.



Swim

Massage

This is a unique procedure for both treatment and prevention of various diseases. ... Classical massage is carried out using four basic techniques, namely: stroking, rubbing, vibration, kneading.



Coach

A specialist who develops sports programs for a client and monitors the correctness of his exercise. Unlike a sports trainer, he does not achieve the maximum sports result, but ensures the general physical fitness of a person.
